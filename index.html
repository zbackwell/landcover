Land Cover Change in the Contiguous U.S.

This map uses and adapts multiple years of land cover data from the National Land Cover Database. Pixel area is 1002m to limit data size but data may still take longer to load depending on the size of your selected state.
Disclaimer: Avoid loading multiple states at a time. After viewing a state, please click "Reset Map" to remove the loaded data and prevent freezing. Current states with data include Oregon, California, Texas, and Louisiana.

Data Sources:
Land Cover Data: National Land Cover Database
Population and State Data: United States Census Bureau
Wildfire Acres Burned: Oregon Department of Forestry, California Department of Forestry and Fire Protection
